Corrugated Boxes

The standard type of box. A generic and plain style of box that can come in various shapes and sizes, it's best suited for general use (e.g. moving boxes, storage boxes, packing boxes, etc).

Gaylord Boxes

A larger specialty type of corrugated box. Gaylord boxes are generally used for collection of recyclable materials (plastic, metal, cardboard, etc).

Wax Boxes

A specialty type of box generally made of multi-layered corrugated cardboard lined with polyethylene wax to prevent damage when wet. Wax boxes are generally used for carrying "wet" items such as produce and seafood.
